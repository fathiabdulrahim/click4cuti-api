Summary: Click4Cuti is intentionally narrower than MySyarikat — its current users table covers auth, minimal HR profile, and the inputs needed for EA 1955 leave calculation. The biggest gaps for the leave domain are: NRIC, Date of Birth, Marital Status, Employee Type/Probation (affects EA 1955 eligibility), OKU Status, two-level approval hierarchy, family members table (compassionate leave), and emergency contact. Payroll, Claims, Attendance, Training, Equipment, and the App Settings are out of scope for the leave management system.
